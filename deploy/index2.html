Our Story

Our hope is that Vault + Vine becomes an urban oasis in the East Falls neighborhood of Philadelphia; an atmosphere of abundance and respite; a space for beauty, whimsy, and rejuvenation.

Vault + Vine is the re-envisioning of Peicha Changâ€™s Falls Flowers. Peicha and her team have outgrown the space and have committed to their new location on Midvale to offer an immersive retail experience while providing the community with a cafe, gathering place, and small scale event venue.

Local creators, whose hands are part of every process of making their goods, produce many of the objects we carry. The seasonal flowers we use in our arrangements are grown in and around Philadelphia at small farms, and are harvested and delivered to us by the same farmers who planted the seeds.

Why We're Different

Falls Flowers was the first floral-retail and event design studio to work sustainably and Vault + Vine aims to continue that legacy. We strive to do what a good business should do: support our community, buy and source locally, pay our employees well, and follow high standards of sustainability.

Through volunteerism, financial support, involvement in community groups, and providing a safe space for all to gather, we aim to provide the best of what the world has to offer to you. We do this by working with our suppliers and vendors to hold ourselves to higher standards.

Meet the Founder

Peicha

Peicha Chang is the founder of Vault + Vine. In 2009, she founded Falls Flowers, a floral design shop in East Falls, before transitioning the business to Vault + Vine in 2017. Before Falls Flowers, Peicha was a designer and manager at Bow Street Flowers in Boston, Massachusetts. At Bow Street, Peicha honed her design sensibilities and discovered her love of garden style design. Peicha is a graduate of Smith College in Northampton, Massachusetts. She lives with her family in East Falls, where she is able to walk to work and share her lifelong love of plants and flowers with her community.
